Most finance apps were designed for a world of fixed paychecks and predictable routines. But today, many people earn through side income, freelancing, creator revenue, online business, commissions, and multiple streams at once. Filo was created to help you understand what’s coming in, what’s safe to spend, and where you’re growing. We believe money tools should feel calm, modern, and built around how your life works.
